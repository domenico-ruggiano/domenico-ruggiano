## 🚀 About Me

Full-stack developer with a deep passion for artificial intelligence applied to compliance and RegTech. In 2024 I founded **TechAI Lab S.r.l. Società Benefit** in Milan, where I lead technical development and product strategy.

I turn complex regulations — GDPR, AI Act, DORA, EU Pay Transparency Directive — into intuitive and accessible software solutions for Italian and European SMEs.

## 🏗️ What I'm Building

### [CertAI](https://www.certai.it?utm_source=github_dom&utm_medium=profile) — *AI-Powered Compliance Platform*
> An intelligent platform for managing ISO certifications and regulatory compliance. Leverages advanced RAG architectures to analyze documents, generate gap analyses, and guide companies toward full compliance.

### [Cedolingo](https://www.cedolingo.com?utm_source=github_dom&utm_medium=profile) — *Payroll Verification Tool*
> A payroll verification tool that helps workers understand and validate every line of their payslip, bringing transparency where it's often lacking.

### [DirettivaTrasparenza.it](https://www.direttivatrasparenza.it?utm_source=github_dom&utm_medium=profile) — *EU Pay Transparency Compliance*
> A SaaS solution helping companies comply with the EU Pay Transparency Directive (2023/970).
